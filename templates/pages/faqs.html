These are different techniques used to detect manipulations:
  • ELA (Error Level Analysis): Identifies areas of an image with different compression levels, which can indicate modification.
  • Noise Analysis: Examines the noise patterns across an image, as manipulations often disrupt these patterns.
  • Metadata Analysis: Checks the file's embedded information (like camera model, software used) for inconsistencies.
  • Copy-Move Detection: Looks for duplicated regions within an image, a common sign of tampering.
